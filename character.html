"I know that strength isn't everything, but in Talos-II, you can't live without it."

As a young mercenary living in the fringes of civilization, Wulfgard is already very familiar with the darkest aspects of humanity. Years of vagabond life also gave him a maturity that goes far beyond his age.
A former member of the Landbreaker clan known as the Pack, Wulfgard can be a reliable and trustworthy friend ... if you can earn his trust. However, this is no easy feat.

Xaihi

Order of Steel Oath

"The angels I know definitely look nothing like this."

Very few people know anything about Xaihi's past. They only heard that she came from an isolated and mysterious place where people have an odd reverence for technology.
Despite her appearances, the way that Xaihi talks and acts is hardly typical of a girl who grew up in a cloister. One must get used to her vast and seemingly trivial knowledge as well as her tendency to jump from one strange topic to the next. Xaihi herself, however, is oblivious to these issues.
